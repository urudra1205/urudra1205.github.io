ABOUT ME

The story behind
the lens

Hey, I'm Akshat — a visual storyteller based in USA with a passion for capturing raw, authentic moments that transcend time. My journey began with a borrowed camera and an insatiable curiosity for the world around me.

Today, I specialize in fashion editorials, music videos, and brand campaigns that push creative boundaries. My work is characterized by a distinctive vintage aesthetic — think rich tones, cinematic compositions, and an emotional depth that resonates with viewers.

When I'm not behind the camera, you'll find me exploring analog photography, collecting vinyl records, or searching for something perfect. Every project I take on is an opportunity to create something meaningful — a visual legacy that will stand the test of time.
